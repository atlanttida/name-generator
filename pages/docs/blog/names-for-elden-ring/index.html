Elden Ring Nicknames: Names for Your Tarnished Champion

Welcome, fellow Tarnished. You’ve risen from the dead, you’ve seen the majesty of the Erdtree, and you’re ready to carve your path through the Lands Between. Whether you're a seasoned player eagerly diving into the Nightreign expansion or a newcomer taking your first steps into Limgrave, one of the first and most crucial decisions you'll make is your character's name. A great name sets the tone for your entire adventure, defining your legend before you even defeat your first boss.

Your nickname is your identity. It’s what other players will see when you’re summoned for jolly co-operation or when you invade their world seeking a duel. It adds a layer of role-playing and immersion that makes your journey to become the Elden Lord that much more personal. Stuck on what to call your new Wretch or Astrologer? Don’t you worry. We’ve journeyed through the lore, battled the demigods, and returned with a treasure trove of Elden Ring nicknames perfect for your next playthrough.

Crafting Your Own Legend

The perfect name might not be on a list—it might be one you create yourself. A great way to do this is by combining two powerful words, using a title, or alluding to your character's build.

  • Combine Lore Terms: Godskin + Hunter = Godskin Hunter.
  • Use a Title: The + Adjective + Noun = The Crimson Blade.
  • Reference Your Build: Faithful for a faith build, Quick-Blade for a dexterity build.
