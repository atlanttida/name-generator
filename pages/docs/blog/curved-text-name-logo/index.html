Fonts say a lot about your style. Here are some themes and suggestions:

  • Funny/Casual: Use handwritten or bubbly fonts like “Baloo” or “Comic Neue”
  • Minimalist/Pro Look: Clean fonts like “Montserrat” or “Open Sans”
