Star Wars Symbols: The Ultimate Guide to Their Meanings

From the emblem of the Rebel Alliance to the insignia of the Galactic Empire, Star Wars symbols are rich with meaning. This guide explores the most iconic symbols from the galaxy far, far away and the stories they represent.

The Star Wars galaxy is defined by a wide array of symbols that represent various governments, Force-based religions, and warrior cultures. Below are the main symbols categorized by their significance in the universe.

Rebel Alliance Starbird

A stylized firebird representing hope and freedom. It was adopted by the rebellion against the Empire and later influenced the New Republic and Resistance logos.

Galactic Empire Crest

This six-spoked cog symbolizes Imperial control, machinery, and authoritarian power under Emperor Palpatine's rule.

Galactic Republic Symbol

An eight-spoked cog representing the unity and democracy of the Republic during the Clone Wars, often associated with the Jedi as guardians.

First Order Symbol

A 16-rayed hexagon signifying a militaristic resurgence of Imperial ideology, embodying the dark side's quest for control in the sequel trilogy.

Separatist Symbol (CIS)

The hexagonal emblem of the Confederacy of Independent Systems, representing the secessionist movement and droid army led by Count Dooku.

New Republic Symbol

The Rebel Starbird encircled by a sunburst, symbolizing rebirth, peace, and democratic renewal after the fall of the Empire.

Jedi Order Crest

Featuring wings and a central lightsaber-like beacon, this crest symbolizes the Jedi's role as guardians of peace, hope, and the light side of the Force.

Sith Symbol

Angular, red designs with spirals and runes represent chaos, power, and the dark side, often used in Sith architecture and tattoos.

Sith Eternal Symbol

This diamond within a notched circle denotes the cult that worshipped the Sith and orchestrated Emperor Palpatine's resurrection on Exegol.

Mandalorian Symbol (Mythosaur Skull)

The skull of the ancient Mythosaur represents the warrior culture, strength, and heritage of the Mandalorian people.

Death Watch Symbol

A stylized diving shriek-hawk, this is the emblem of the militant Mandalorian splinter group that sought to restore ancient warrior traditions.

Mudhorn Symbol

The sigil of Clan Mudhorn, founded by Din Djarin and Grogu, representing the bond of found family and protection.

Black Sun Symbol

A sun-like emblem representing the powerful and influential crime syndicate involved in smuggling and espionage across the galaxy.

Desilijic Clan Symbol

The anchor-like insignia of Jabba the Hutt's powerful crime family, symbolizing the Hutt cartel's dominance in the Outer Rim.

Chiss Ascendancy Symbol

Interconnected circles representing the government of the Chiss in the Unknown Regions, famously led by Grand Admiral Thrawn.

Ahsoka Tano's Symbol

Patterns mimicking Ahsoka's distinctive facial markings, representing her journey from Padawan to rebel operative "Fulcrum."

Government and Faction Symbols

  • Rebel Alliance Starbird: This stylized firebird represents hope, freedom, and resistance against tyranny. It originated from Sabine Wren's graffiti and was later adopted by the New Republic and the Resistance.
  • Galactic Empire Crest: A six-spoked cog symbolizing control, machinery, and uniformity. It evolved from the Republic's symbol to enforce imperial dominance.
  • Galactic Republic Symbol: An eight-spoked cog representing unity, democracy, and justice. It is closely associated with the Jedi's role as guardians during the Clone Wars.
  • First Order Symbol: A hexagon containing 16 inward-pointing rays, signifying a militaristic resurgence of imperial ideology and the dark side.
  • New Republic Symbol: This emblem features the Rebel Starbird encircled by a sunburst, symbolizing rebirth and democratic renewal after the Empire's fall.
  • Confederacy of Independent Systems (Separatist Symbol): A hexagonal emblem representing secession and independence for the droid army and its allies.
  • High Republic and Old Republic Symbols: The High Republic uses nine rays fanning from a spear to denote prosperity and justice, while the Old Republic uses a bird in flight to represent ancient guardianship.

Jedi and Sith Order Symbols

  • Jedi Order Crest: Composed of wings with a central beacon or lightsaber, this symbol signifies peace, balance, and the guardianship of the light side.

Mandalorian and Clan Symbols

  • Mythosaur Skull: The primary Mandalorian symbol, featuring a skull with protruding jawbones, representing strength, heritage, and warrior culture.
  • Mudhorn Symbol: The signet for Clan Mudhorn (Din Djarin and Grogu), representing protection and found family.
  • Death Watch Symbol: A stylized diving shriek-hawk used by the Mandalorian splinter group seeking to return to traditional warrior ways.
